Epic Eye Tracker

Team Epic was formed in February 2014 with the founding members of Samuel Di Poala, Paul Uncangco, Matthew Robertson, and Michael Schirmer.They started with a vision to make computing convenient and accessible to everyone without the crippling cost of expensive solutions,or locked to any particular device. Thus, the idea of the lightweight, portable, and affordable device called the "Epic Eye Tracker".The team is looking to have the Epic Eye Tracker ship during Spring 2014.

Specs

  • Maximum Supported Resolution: 7680x4320 (8K)
  • Device Weight: 200g
  • Supported Operating Systems: Windows 7, Windows 8, Mac OSX, Linux, Unix, Android, IOS
