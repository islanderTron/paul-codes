How to Use

This device will not pick up on blinking involuntarily. It will only look for specific expressed eye signs.

Left Click: Blink left eye

Right Click: Blink right eye

Middle Click: Blink both eyes (for an extended period of time)

Virtual Keyboard:

When the Epic Eyes Tracker is installed, it comes with a virtual keyboard app that you can overlay on your screen. Once this program is accessed, you may click each key individually to type messages (Or right click to chain together keys such as CTRL+ALT+DEL).

Example of Shortcuts That Can Be Found on the Keyboard:

  • CTRL+C: Copy
  • CTRL+X: Cut
  • CTRL+V: Paste
  • CTRL+Z: Undo
  • CTRL+A: Select All
